## Description｜說明  
目前美國與歐盟一直在持續針對人工智慧立法規範，涵蓋的範圍越來越廣，並涉及到一般使用者的各種 AI 應用。  
The United States and the European Union are continuously developing regulatory frameworks for artificial intelligence, with expanding scope that increasingly affects various AI applications used by the general public.  

這裡整理了目前為止所有 AI 的相關法律資訊，供各位參考。  
This section compiles the latest AI-related legal information available to date for your reference.  

不論自己或所在的組織或企業，都要注意 AI 應用可能帶來的法律風險。  
Whether as individuals or as part of an organization or enterprise, it is important to be aware of the potential legal risks associated with AI applications.
